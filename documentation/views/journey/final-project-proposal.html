Final Project proposal

Nature, science, technology, digital fabrication, electronics, networking, data visualisation: the internet of plants

"Sensitive plants network micro environment "

After expending many hours the last month reading and jumping throw a large amount interesting articles and books, which ones could clarify my vision of how to make tangible my final project and at the same time to satisfy my actual curiosities, finally I decided to follow my initial and personal interests to mix multimedia areas and be involved with nature choosing plants as my main inspiration and try to use them as in input sensor device.

Mind mapping: Simple map mind of my interests and why I decided to work on.

First concept ideas

"plants can monitor 15 chemical and physical parameters"
Stefano Mancuso - TED Conference
http://www.ted.com/talks/stefano_mancuso_the_roots_of_plant_intelligence.html

"Plant sense" pretend to be an experimental interactive bio micro environment to monitor plants as sensor input device. The micro environment installation will have 3 plants. Each one that will be connected as a grid of electrical network into the soil by a recipient formed by coil sewed in a special fabric to retain water. It will also have a series of input and output devices to allow the interaction with users when plants receives an input signal and, to be able to change the environment of the plant for specific studies .

Based on a network of monitored sensitive plants or cyborg plants, how ever the online reporters call this movement today, throw a "DIY" philosophy at my Fab Academy journey, I will pretend to create a bunch of hardware and a software to gather data from a grid of embedded sensors network.
